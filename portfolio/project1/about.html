CompuZone, Inc. was established in 1992. We are locally owned and operated. With 3 locations in Charleston we are convienently located to your home or business. We are your Complete Technology Solutions Provider.

We are celebrating over 12 years in the Charleston area with many thanks to our customers and local supporting businesses.

Our goal is to provide you with quality components offering superior customer support and technology solutions at a price that is affordable with little to no down time. We understand how important it is to have a system in place whether it be your home or office that runs efficiently at all times. We offer in house technical support on all computers and on-site services to include maintenance, repair, troubleshooting and network configurations.

CompuZone is your one stop shop for all of your technology solutions. We look forward to providing you with the best service in Charleston.
